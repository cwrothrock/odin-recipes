Tiger Butter

Description

Do you like peanut butter and chocolate? This easy fudge is the best combination of the two.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb white almond bark chocolate
  • 3/4 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 pkg (~10 oz) Reece's peanut butter chips
  • 1/2 pkg (~6 oz) semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Melt the first three ingredients, stirring occasionally until smooth.
  2. Melt the semi-sweet chips separately.
  3. Line a baking sheet or pan of your choosing (depending on desired thickness) with foil.
  4. Pour the melted mixture into the pan.
  5. Drizzle the melted semi-sweet chocolate over the mixture and swirl with a knife, careful not to overmix.
  6. Refrigerate until set, then break/cut into pieces and store in sealed container(s).
